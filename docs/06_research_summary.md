## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### High Priority Risks

#### 1. Copyright & Legal Risks
**Risk:** AI-generated music copyright uncertainty
**Likelihood:** Medium
**Impact:** High
**Mitigation:**
- Consult with music licensing attorneys
- Implement clear terms of service
- Use original AI models (HeartMuLa) with proper licensing
- Provide commercial rights for generated content

#### 2. Technical Performance
**Risk:** AI model quality inconsistent with commercial standards
**Likelihood:** Medium
**Impact:** High
**Mitigation:**
- Implement quality control and filtering
- Provide multiple AI providers (HeartMuLa + Suno fallback)
- Continuous model improvement and training
- User feedback loop for quality improvement

#### 3. Market Competition
**Risk:** Large tech companies entering the space
**Likelihood:** Medium
**Impact:** Medium
**Mitigation:**

- Build strong user community and brand
- Develop unique learning capabilities
- Create ecosystem value (marketplace, templates)

**Likelihood:** High
